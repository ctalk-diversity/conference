Call for Speakers: Environment

Theme: Environment

May 27, 2026 Open Call

Theme Overview

We are seeking speakers for a Cultural Talk session on environment. This open call welcomes perspectives on how cultural practices, social change, innovation, and shared responsibility shape the way communities understand and respond to environmental issues.

Speakers may connect environmental sustainability with tradition, globalization, climate responsibility, everyday choices, or community action.

Topics may include:

  • The role of traditional culture in environmental sustainability
  • Eco-friendly innovations in modern society
  • The intersection of globalization and environmental preservation
  • Climate change and cultural responsibility

Event Format

  • Each speaker delivers a 10-minute speech, followed by a 20+ minute discussion with all participants.
