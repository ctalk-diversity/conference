Conference Overview

The 28th Cultural Talk looks at career breaks through the lens of gender, work, and everyday realities across Asia and beyond. The session centers on how expectations around care, ambition, family, and professional growth can shape the choices and constraints women face over the course of a career.

Anupama S Kapoor will lead the discussion with the topic, "Can Women Have It All? Gender, Work & Reality in Asia (India & Beyond)." Detailed speaker profile content will be added once the remaining materials are ready.
